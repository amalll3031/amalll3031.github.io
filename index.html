How it Works

Three steps to a better hire.

ADAQ eliminates guesswork. Every decision is backed by a structured, objective assessment — not gut feeling.

01
Run the Interview
Schedule an AI-assisted video interview. The system records responses, analyzes verbal content, tone, and behavioral signals simultaneously.
02
Get the Report
Within minutes, receive a full competency report: 6 dynamic dimensions scored 1–5, bias-reduced and benchmarked against your role criteria.
03
Hire with Confidence
Compare top candidates side by side on a unified scorecard. Every recommendation is explainable, auditable, and compliant with Nitaqat requirements.
